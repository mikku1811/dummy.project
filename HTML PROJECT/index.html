खाटू श्याम भक्त मण्डल

Khatushyam is a temple situated in sikar district of Rajasthan,India. In Hinduism, Khatushyam is a name and manifestation of Barbareek, grandson of Bhim and son of Hidimba. This manifestation is especially popular in the Indian state of Rajasthan and Haryana. The original Sanskrit name Barbarīka is often replaced in Rajasthan by the Hindi version, Barbarīk, often written as Barbareek.
